# OPERATION BLACKSITE — Speedrun Notes

Campaign timer starts on your **first input in Mission 1** and stops when you touch the
helipad at the end of Mission 4. Pause (Esc) freezes it; death does not. Sub-18:00 = S rank.

## Core tech

### Slide-hopping (the movement tech)
- Sprint (Shift + W) → **C** to slide. The slide boosts you to ~11.5 m/s vs 8.0 sprint and
  bleeds speed slowly.
- **Jump during the slide.** Airborne speed is fully preserved — air control only steers,
  it never slows you below the speed you launched with.
- On landing, immediately slide again (you keep sprint state). Chain: sprint → slide → hop →
  land → slide → hop. Sustained ~10–11 m/s, roughly **35% faster than sprinting**.
- Slides also make enemies miss more (movement-based dodge bonus is highest while sliding).

### Reload cancel
The reload animation commits ammo only at the very end. Swap weapons (1/2/3 or scroll) to
cancel the recovery the moment you don't need the mag — or better, never reload mid-fight:
swap rifle → pistol → rifle. The pistol's 0.18 s swap is the fastest "reload" in the game.

### Hold-E objectives
Interact progress **pauses** when you release E or leave the radius — it never resets.
You can tap a console, fight, and come back.

### General
- Radio dialogue never blocks anything. Press F to skip lines (audio only — no time save,
  it's for your sanity on grinds).
- Enemies telegraph 0.4 s (weapon raise) before firing. You can sprint past almost every
  fight; only scripted defends and the boss are mandatory kills.
- HP regenerates after 4 s out of combat. Deaths respawn at the checkpoint with the timer
  running — a death is usually a 10–30 s loss, never a reset.

## Mission routes

### M1 — Cold Open (target 4:00, optimal ~2:30)
- Slide-hop straight up the middle lane of the container yard. All four dock grunts are
  skippable — they need line of sight + 0.4 s telegraph; you're gone before the burst.
- Breach (1.2 s hold) → the 3 warehouse grunts spawn at the back. Shotgun-dash to the
  console; you can kill all three during the 8 s download from the console cage (they
  funnel between the crates).
- Exfil out the east door, one slide-hop to the crane pad. The two exfil spawns can be
  ignored entirely.

### M2 — Switchyard (target 5:00, optimal ~3:20; 60 s is fixed)
- **Tank order: WEST → NORTH → EAST.** West tank (-52, 21) is nearest the spawn-side rail
  lanes; the north tank (0, -55) chains into the east tank (52, 5) on the return diagonal,
  which drops you near the signal tower. East-first wastes the longest leg twice.
- The shed grunt and the platform heavy never need to die. The heavy is slow — give him
  10 m and he can't land a hit on a slider.
- The 60 s tower hold is the fixed cost of the mission. Fight from the platform — waves
  funnel along the railcars. Pre-pump the shotgun before each wave.
- The instant the hold ends, slide-hop the straight line to the engine at the north
  platform (~7 s).

### M3 — Black Rain (target 6:00, optimal ~3:30)
- Pure straight line. Every fight is skippable except what physically blocks the boxcar
  doorways — shotgun one-taps grunts at door range; headshot (2×) makes elites two-taps.
- **Skip the mounted gun.** It exists for casual play; mounting costs more time than it
  saves. Push past the flatbed.
- The boxcar roofs are walkable: jump from crates onto a roof and slide-hop the roofline to
  bypass two interior fights entirely (storm winds are cosmetic — no knock-off).
- Engine console is a 4 s hold. The story line plays over your run — don't stop.

### M4 — Vault (target 7:00, optimal ~4:30)
- **Switch order: EAST first, then WEST** if you enter the hub hugging the right wall
  (your descent ramp exits on the west side, so cross once instead of twice... check your
  own line — the rooms are symmetric; the rule is just *never re-cross the hub*).
- **Boss (Commander Roth):** shield blocks all body/head damage. The generator is the
  orange backpack — he turns slowly (deliberately slower than your slide), so slide past
  his shoulder at close range and unload into the pack. Pack breaks → dump the rifle into
  his body. Each ~200 hp phase re-shields him and spawns adds (grunts, then elites).
  **Ignore the adds** — they despawn nothing, but the mission ends when Roth dies, and the
  escape spawns fresh enemies anyway. Three pack-breaks + three damage dumps ≈ 45–60 s.
- Grab BLACKSITE (2 s hold) → 90 s escape. The route back up is the route you came down.
  A clean slide-hop chain does it in ~35 s; the timer is generous so deaths in the arena
  don't soft-lock the run. Escape spawns can all be sprinted past.
